font usage-
Chivo (Sans-serif)– Use for:
* **Headings (h1–h4)** — clean, bold, and readable
* **Navbar links**
* **Buttons**

Lora (Serif)– Use for:
* **Body text / paragraphs**
* **"About Me" section**
* **Project descriptions**
* **Footer text**
